About Us

We are the world's most diverse university

We are Disciple University to the world for every type of adult learner. We serve students seeking part-time, online courses and nonresidential programs to advance their career or pursue an academic passion.

We are a fully accredited Disciple school. Our degrees and certificates are adorned with the Disciple University insignia. They carry the weight of that lineage. Our graduates walk at University commencement and become members of the Disciple Alumni Association.

As one of 12 degree-granting institutions at Disciple University, we teach to the largest and most eclectic student body. Our students come to us from every time zone, every culture and career background, every age from 18 to 89.

Our students have one thing in common: the motivation to take the next challenging step in their lives.
